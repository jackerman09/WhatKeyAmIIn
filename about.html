About Us

What Key Am I In? is all about helping you become a better musician without having to sweat the small stuff. Why waste time searching the web or poring over books just to figure out what key to rip a sweet solo in when your buddy gives you a chord progression?

With WKAII, just plug in the chords, and let it do the work for you!

Check out our newest feature, the keyBoard, which we've added to complement the fretBoard, and to hopefully open up the site to even more musicians.

For our next update, look for the addition of more keys (which was one of the most requested features after the tuner and the keyBoard). Don't forget to keep your eyes peeled for even more new features, coming soon!

Feel free to reach out to info@WhatKeyAmIIn.com with any questions, comments or suggestions!
